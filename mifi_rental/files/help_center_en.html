1. How to get started with G4?

1. Download GlocalMe APP (Andriod and IOS available)

2. Create your GlocalMe account through email or phone number

3. Press and hold the Power button for 3 seconds until the G4 turns on, tap Bind Now button, and scan the QR code displayed in “Scan to bind”

4. Login to your GlocalMe account on the phone, go to My device > Bind device,and then scan the QR code on the G4 screen (Go to More > QR Code on the G4)

5. Reboot G4 or tap Connect Now button, use your phone to find and select the G4 Wi-Fi network name (GlocalMe_XXXXX) then connect to GlocalMe Wi-Fi Networks

2. How long does it take to fully charge the G4?

Approximately 3.5 hours.

3. How long can G2 work continuously?

Depending on usage, you can expect a fully charged battery to last 12 hours approximately.

4. How many devices can be connected with G4 at the same time?

Up to 5 devices can be supported by G4 at the same time.

5. How to change Wi-Fi name and password?

From the G4 home screen, tap the hotspot information and then you can change the Wi-Fi name and Wi-Fi password in “Wi-Fi Manager” page.

6. How to use G4 as a powerbank?

The OTG cable allows you use your G4 to charge a mobile phone. G4 can be used as power bank when it is powered on and has over 30% battery.

Please check product manual for more informaitons.

1. What is GlocalMe?

GlocalMe is a kind of global mobile Internet access service. GlocalMe device is a portable SIM-free Wi-Fi hotspot that allows Internet connection in over 100 countries and regions.

2. How to access GlocalMe service?

If you have a GlocalMe device, you need to create a GlocalMe account first. After you recharge the account, you can log in to the device and connect it to GlocalMe network.

3. Do I need to have a GlocalMe account?

Yes. Users are required to log in with GlocalMe account to access the WiFi service.

4. Does G4 support 4G?

Yes, GlocalMe G4 support 4G.

5. How do I check the data usage?

Login to GlocalMe App or www.glocalme.com.

6. What should I do if my device is lost?

Please log GlocalMe APP with your account immediately, change your password and unlink your account from the device. Then your balance will be protected. Besides, you can also notify UCL customer service team.

More details, please visit www.glocalme.com
